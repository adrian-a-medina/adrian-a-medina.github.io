As a Research Instructor for Harvard Summer School's MBB S-102 course, I teach and advise students' research experience while overseeing their lab work. I introduce students to the various methods, techniques, and theories used by psychologists and neuroscientists in their study of the brain, behavior, cognition, and learning. As a Research Instructor, I guide students in formulating hypotheses, designing experiments, collecting and analyzing data, and presenting their findings to myself and their class. I am also responsible for designing laboratory materials, providing individualized feedback on their assignments, and teaching weekly lab sections.
